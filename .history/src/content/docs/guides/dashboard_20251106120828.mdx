### 1. Login Page

After creating your admin account during onboarding, go to the Login Page and sign in using your credentials.

Once logged in successfully, you will be redirected to your Dashboard, where you can manage WhatsApp campaigns, view message statistics, and access all available system features.

### 2. Add Your First Device

After logging in, navigate to the Devices section from the dashboard menu.

Click “Add Device”, then enter your mobile number (the one with WhatsApp installed).
Once submitted, the system will create and link your device.

List with your registered mobile number.

Next to it, you’ll see an “Action” button labeled QR Code.

5 WhatsApp Connection Confirmation

Once your device is successfully connected, you will see a confirmation message — “Connected Successfully” — displayed on your dashboard.

Your WhatsApp profile image will also appear, confirming that the connection is active.
From this point, your account is ready to send and manage WhatsApp messages directly from the dashboard.

6 Adding Contacts

You can easily add multiple contacts at once using a bulk CSV import.

Navigate to the Contacts section in your dashboard.

Click “Import Contacts.”

Select your CSV file containing the contact data.

Click Upload to import all contacts into the system.

Make sure your CSV file follows the correct format before uploading. (A sample CSV format is attached for reference.)

7 Creating a Campaign

You can send bulk WhatsApp messages by creating a campaign.

Navigate to the Campaigns section in your dashboard.

Click “Create Campaign.”

Enter a Campaign Name.

Select the Group or contact list you want to send messages to.

Type your Message in the text box.

(Optional) Add emojis or attachments such as images to personalize your message.

Choose the Sender Number from the available devices.

Click “Create Campaign” to launch your bulk message campaign.

Your campaign will now start sending messages automatically based on your selected configuration.
