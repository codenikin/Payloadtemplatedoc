### 1. Login Page

After creating your admin account during onboarding, go to the Login Page and sign in using your credentials.

Once logged in successfully, you will be redirected to your Dashboard, where you can manage WhatsApp campaigns, view message statistics, and access all available system features.

### 2. Add Your First Device

After logging in, navigate to the Devices section from the dashboard menu.

Click “Add Device”, then enter your mobile number (the one with WhatsApp installed).
Once submitted, the system will create and link your device.

### 3. Connect Your Device

After creating your device, it will appear in the Device
List with your registered mobile number.

Next to it, you’ll see an “Action” button labeled QR Code.
Click the QR Code button — a WhatsApp Web QR code will appear on the screen.
